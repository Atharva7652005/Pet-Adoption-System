Frequently Asked Questions

To adopt a pet, browse our available pets, submit an adoption application, and our team will review your application. If approved, you'll be invited for a meet and greet with your potential new pet.

Adoption fees vary depending on the type and age of the pet. Dogs typically range from $150-$300, cats from $75-$150, and small animals from $25-$75. All adoption fees include vaccinations, spay/neuter, and microchipping.

We're always looking for volunteers and foster families! Please fill out our volunteer application form or foster application form on our website, and our volunteer coordinator will contact you with more information.

The adoption process typically takes 3-7 days from application to bringing your pet home. This includes application review, reference checks, meet and greet, and finalizing the adoption paperwork.
